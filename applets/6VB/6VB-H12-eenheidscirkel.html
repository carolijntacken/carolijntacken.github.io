Exacte waarden in de eenheidscirkel

Als je in de eenheidscirkel de sinus en cosinus van de basishoeken kent, weet je de rest ook. In deze applet zie je wat de samenhang is tussen de verschillende hoeken.

\(\sin(0)=\sin(\pi)=\sin(2\pi)=0\)
\(\cos(0)=\cos(2\pi)=1\) \(\cos(\pi)=-1\)
\(\sin(\frac16\pi)=\sin(\frac56\pi)=\frac12\) \(\sin(1\frac16\pi)=\sin(1\frac56\pi)=-\frac12\)
\(\cos(\frac16\pi)=\cos(1\frac56\pi)=\frac12\sqrt{3}\) \(\cos(\frac56\pi)=\cos(1\frac16\pi)=-\frac12\sqrt{3}\)
\(\sin(\frac14\pi)=\sin(\frac34\pi)=\frac12\sqrt{2}\) \(\sin(1\frac14\pi)=\sin(1\frac34\pi)=-\frac12\sqrt{2}\)
\(\cos(\frac14\pi)=\cos(1\frac34\pi)=\frac12\sqrt2\) \(\cos(\frac34\pi)=\cos(1\frac14\pi)=-\frac12\sqrt2\)
\(\sin(\frac13\pi)=\sin(\frac23\pi)=\frac12\sqrt{3}\) \(\sin(\frac43\pi)=\sin(\frac53\pi)=-\frac12\sqrt{3}\)
\(\cos(\frac13\pi)=\cos(\frac53\pi)=\frac12\) \(\cos(\frac23\pi)=\cos(\frac43\pi)=-\frac12\)
\(\sin(\frac12\pi)=1\) \(\sin(1\frac12\pi)=-1\)
\(\cos(\frac12\pi)=\cos(1\frac12\pi)=0\)
